Classic Omelette

Ingredients

Here we are going to learn how to prepare an easy omelette.

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • Optional fillings: cheese, ham, vegetables
  1. Beat the eggs and milk together in a bowl. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and cook until the edges to start the set.
  3. Add any optional fillings to one half of the omelette.
  4. Fold the omelette in half and cook for another minute or until fully set.
  5. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve hot.
